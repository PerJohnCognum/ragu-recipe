Instructions
  1. Finely dice onions, carrots, celery, pancetta, and garlic.
  2. Brown the meat in batches in a large pot over medium heat; set aside.
  3. Sauté the diced vegetables and pancetta until soft and golden.
  4. Return meat to pot, add tomato paste, and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Pour in white wine and let reduce by half.
  6. Add canned tomatoes, bay leaves, and herbs; simmer gently for 3-4 hours.
  7. Stir in whole milk near the end; adjust seasoning.
  8. Serve over fresh pasta or polenta.

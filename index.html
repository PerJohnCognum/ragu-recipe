Italian Ragù Recipe & Shopping List (12 Portions)

Shopping List

Meat (choose one combination):

  • Option 1: 1.5kg Svinebog (pork shoulder) + 1kg Høyrygg (beef chuck)
  • Option 2: 2kg Bibringe (beef short ribs)
  • Option 3: 1.2kg Svinebog + 800g Lammebog (lamb shoulder)

Vegetables & Aromatics:

  • 3 large onions (450g)
  • 4 large carrots (400g)
  • 4 stalks celery (200g)
  • 200g pancetta or bacon
  • 1 head garlic
  • Fresh bay leaves (6–8 leaves)
  • Fresh thyme or rosemary

Pantry Items:

  • 200ml whole milk
  • 400ml dry white wine
  • 800ml beef or vegetable stock
  • 800g canned San Marzano tomatoes
  • 6 tbsp tomato paste (concentrated)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano for serving
Recipe Checklist

Prep (30 minutes):

  1. Cut meat into 3cm chunks (not minced)
  2. Finely dice onions, carrots, celery (soffritto)
  3. Dice pancetta
  4. Crush tomatoes by hand
  5. Measure out wine, milk, stock

Cooking Process (4–5 hours):

Phase 1: Build the Base (20 minutes)

  1. Heat olive oil in heavy-bottomed pot
  2. Brown meat chunks in batches (don’t overcrowd)
  3. Remove meat, set aside
  4. Sauté pancetta until fat renders
  5. Add soffritto, cook until softened (10 minutes)

Phase 2: Deglaze & Combine (15 minutes)

  1. Add tomato paste, cook 2 minutes
  2. Return meat to pot
  3. Add milk, simmer until absorbed
  4. Add wine, simmer until reduced by half
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, bay leaves, herbs

Phase 3: Long Braise (3–4 hours)

  1. Bring to gentle simmer
  2. Cover partially, cook very low heat
  3. Stir every 30 minutes
  4. Add stock gradually as needed
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning in final hour
  6. Sauce ready when meat shreds easily

Final Check:

  1. Meat breaks into fibrous strands
  2. Sauce coats spoon but isn’t dry
  3. Rich, deep color
  4. No raw wine taste
Serving Notes
  • Serve with fresh tagliatelle or pappardelle
  • Top with grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Can be made 1–2 days ahead (improves flavor)
  • Freezes well for up to 3 months
